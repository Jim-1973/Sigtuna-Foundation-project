About Us

The Sigtuna Foundation is the start of another exciting chapter in Kenyan football. The new charity will support the development of football facilities through investment, assistance and guidance.

It will deliver the Football Kenya Federation’ (FKF) commitment to develop inspirational fit-for-future football facilities that will improve and grow Kenyan football on and off the pitch and is preparing to undertake the largest programme of funding in the history of Kenyan football.

The standard of football facilities across Kenya, particularly at grassroots level, requires coordinated long-term investment to deliver the most accessible and appropriate environments for current and future members of the Kenyans football family.

The Sigtuna Foundation will play a leading role in addressing this challenge by delivering a series of facility development programmes that will develop outstanding football facilities, resulting in more players and better experiences – helping improve the health and wellbeing of a nation.

The priorities of the Sigtuna Foundation will be to support projects that:

  1. Enable stronger and sustainable community focussed clubs.
  2. Grow opportunities and improve experiences for women and girls.
  3. Support wider community needs and tackle inequalities.

David Onyango, The Founder and Head of Facility Investment and Operations at the Sigtuna Foundation, said: “The launch of the Sigtuna Foundation is a significant development for Kenyan football. “Access to appropriate and high-quality facilities are integral to the experiences of every player, coach, volunteer and referee in Kenya.
